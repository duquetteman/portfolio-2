About Me

Welcome to my profile page! I am new to the world of web development but I am excited to learn and create. As a developer, I wanted to create a portfolio website so that I can showcase my creations to future employers. I also wanted this to serve as a brief introduction to who I am and what type of employment I am looking for.

My education and professional experiences cover a broad spectrum. I have a BS in Psychology from Iowa State University, and an MBA with a Finance emphasis from Ashford University. I have worked for Apple for the last 9 years as a Senior Technical Advisor and Team Manager Support. Prior to that, I worked for John Deere Financial, supporting commercial loan customers. Before that, I was with AT&T in a role that focused primarily on customer support and sales, with team management duties assigned as needed.

Learning web development has always been a personal goal. Only recently did I decide to make it my new career. I am working through the UT Coding Bootcamp and hope to find gainful employment in the Austin area after graduation. I would like to start in web developmemnt and eventually explore machine learning and data science. I am fascinated by the technologies that make our modern world work.

I live in Pflugerville with my wife and 6 beautiful children. I enjoy spending time with them and watching them grow. We watch movies, take walks, and cook together. Family time is one of life's little pleasures.
